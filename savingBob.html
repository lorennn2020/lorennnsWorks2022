Bob, is my closest friend, we have lived together for 4 years since I found him on the street. Wish he can stay with me for my rest of life.

Jeff has to find a way to cure Bob. Explore the world and collect ingredients to make a magical jelly. Bring his cat back to life and be happy ever after.

The original intention is to combine my previous UIUX and front-end engineers' skill to make a web game that can be played in 5 minutes. Such as Html, Css, and Javascript. From script conception, level design, scene drawing, and program development, to game testing.

A lot of content that I had to give up due to the time limit, which also made me understand more about the reality of game development. Originally hoped that Bob could go to 5 different scenes to explore and collect more intriguing creatures, but as the development time got longer and longer. I had to choose games which are not too difficult to develop. The puzzle game is one of them. The scene is good, and you won't spend too much time on the program.

I spent a lot of time drawing scenes. The most fascinating one is Dr. Scout's studio, the avant-garde color schemes, mysterious creatures, and experimental equipment. I had a lot of fun drawing this.

It took me 3 months to complete, and it was the proudest side project I've ever done.

Thanks to my two friends Yuna Hsu and Jamie Yu for helping me to write the script, code review, and complete this mini game together!
